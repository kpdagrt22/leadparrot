Compact pill status marker with a mono micro-label. Use for lead tier, source type, stage, plan, status.

```jsx
<Badge tone="high">High intent</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent" solid>Reddit</Badge>
<Badge tone="medium">Solution-aware</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral · accent · high · medium · low · danger · info`. `solid` fills the badge for stronger emphasis. Keep labels to 1–2 words.
